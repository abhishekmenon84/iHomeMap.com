LiDAR Precision

Apple RoomPlan + LiDAR detects walls, doors, windows, and furniture to centimetre accuracy in real-time. Works in rooms up to ~10m × 10m.

Accuracy degrades near mirrors and glass — the manual correction tools let you fix those areas in seconds.

Manual Correction

Tap any element to fine-tune: adjust wall lengths, flip door swing directions, or choose from 7 door types — swing, sliding, folding, revolving, double swing, shutter, and patio.

Every change is undoable. The canvas updates live as you edit.

PDF Export

Professional multi-page PDFs with labelled rooms, dimension lines, north arrow, scale bar, and your property's full address header.

Share via AirDrop, email, or save to Files. $4.99 CAD per export — no subscription.

3D USDZ Model

Every scan generates a USDZ 3D model for AR preview via QuickLook — share it or open on any Apple device instantly. Always free.

Walk clients through a room virtually before they visit in person.

All floors export together into one structured PDF.
