Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 2026

iHomeMap ("we", "the app") is built by an independent developer. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

Data We Collect

We collect no personal data. The app does not have an account system, does not require sign-in, and does not transmit any data to external servers.

LiDAR & Camera

iHomeMap uses your device's LiDAR scanner and camera exclusively to generate floor plan geometry. This sensor data is processed entirely on-device using Apple's RoomPlan framework and is never stored outside your device or transmitted anywhere.

Scan Data Storage

All floor plan data (room geometry, dimensions, door types, property names) is stored locally on your iPhone or iPad using iOS secure storage. It does not leave your device unless you explicitly export a PDF and share it yourself via AirDrop, email, or Files.

In-App Purchases

PDF export credits are purchased through Apple's App Store using StoreKit. Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. We do not see or store your payment information. Apple's privacy policy applies to all transactions: apple.com/legal/privacy.

Analytics & Advertising

The app contains no analytics SDKs, no advertising SDKs, and no crash reporting services. We do not track usage.

Children

iHomeMap does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes materially, we will update the effective date above and note it in the App Store release notes.

Contact

Questions? Email us at support@ihomemap.com.
