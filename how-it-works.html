How It Works
Four steps. Under 10 minutes.
01

Create a Property

Tap the + button on the home screen. Enter your address and the number of floors. Takes about 30 seconds.

You can add as many properties as you like — iHomeMap stores them all locally on your device, forever, for free.

02

Scan Your Room

Walk slowly around the room, pointing your iPhone at each wall. RoomPlan detects walls, doors, windows, and furniture in real-time.

Most rooms take 1–3 minutes. Large open-plan spaces may take up to 5 minutes.

03

Review & Correct

Tap any element on the floor plan canvas to edit it: adjust wall lengths, change door types, or reposition elements.

Choose from 7 door types: swing, sliding, folding, revolving, double swing, shutter, and patio. Every edit is undoable.

04

Export PDF

Tap Export. Use one $4.99 CAD credit to generate a professional PDF. Preview it in the app, then share via AirDrop, email, or Files.

The PDF includes all rooms on the floor, dimension lines, a north arrow, scale bar, and your property address.
